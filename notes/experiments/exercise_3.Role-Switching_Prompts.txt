Exercise 3: Role-Switching Prompts
Objective: Understand persona and style influence.

 Instructions:
 1. Ask the model to respond as different personas: teacher, villain, scientist,
    poet.
 2. Compare outputs.
 3. Predict the effect of persona before generating and check accuracy.

 Reflection Questions:
 - How does tone change meaning or clarity?
 - How much of the output is content vs. style-driven?
 - Does your expectation of style align with reality?
