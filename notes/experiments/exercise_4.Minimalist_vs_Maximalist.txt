Exercise 4: Minimalist vs. Maximalist
Objective: Find the balance between over-specification and ambiguity.

 Instructions:
 1. Take a moderately complex question.
 2. Create on ultra-short prompt, one highly detailed prompt.
 3. Generate outputs and compare
 4. Iteratively adjust toward a "sweet spot."

 Reflection Questions:
 - When does brevity enhance understanding?
 - When does detail prevent ambiguity?
 - How does iteration help find balance?
